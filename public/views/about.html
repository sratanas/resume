STEPHANIE RATANAS

Hello! I am a writer of words and a writer of code based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA. I have a certificate in Full Stack Engineering from Prime Digital Academy in Minneapolis, and BA in journalism with creative writing minor from DePaul University in Chicago. My writing has covered Chicago ward politics, housing issues, feminism, LGBTQ rights, mental health and other topics too random to continue listing. For over a decade, I worked in the coffee industry as a buyer and in other positions. I wrote about coffee growing communities, the complexities of the supply chain at farm level and how tasty coffee was or was not. You can find some of that writing here.

In the spring of 2016, I was selected as a mentee in the Association of Writers and Writing Programs Writer to Writer mentorship program. The experience deeply influenced my life in ways beyond craft, and I am inspired to pay that inspiration forward. As of March I have become a volunteer mentor for the Minnesota Prison Writing Workshop.

When I am not writing or reading, I enjoy travel, camping in style, watercolor painting, caring for house plants, drinking wine, and dog-centric activities with my border collie, Kevin.
